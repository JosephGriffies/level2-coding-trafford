Drinks

Here at Coffee Fever we offer a wide variety of drinks for any weather or any mood. If its a warm summers day and you need something to help cool off you will be sure to enjoy our iced coffee. Or if its a cold winters morning and you need something to kick start your day the right way you will love our espressos and lattes. For all of our drinks we offer vegan versions as well as none vegan as we believe it is important that everyone can enjoy a good cup of coffee. If you have any allergies or other concerns please do not worry our baristas will tell you all you need to know so that you can have the best time possible at Coffee Fever.
